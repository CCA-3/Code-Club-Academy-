Enhancing Confidence and Independence

Smart VI is an initiative by Code Club Academy focused on improving the lives of visually impaired individuals through innovative assistive technology.


Why Smart VI Matters

Millions face challenges due to visual impairments. Smart VI provides tools that foster independence and improve quality of life, aiming to create a more inclusive world.


Our Flagship Product: Smart VI

Smart VI Attach is a lightweight, portable device designed for real-time assistance and navigation.


Key features include:
  • • Obstacle Detection for safe movement
  • • Emergency Button for quick support
  • • User-Friendly & Adaptable
  • • LED Lighting for low-light navigation
